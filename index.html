iPhone 3g

The iPhone 2G, released in 2007, marked a revolutionary leap in the world of mobile technology. It was Apple's inaugural entry into the smartphone market and set the foundation for the iconic line of devices that followed. The 2G, often referred to as the original iPhone, was a sleek, minimalist marvel. Its groundbreaking touch screen interface, a concept that was relatively novel at the time, forever altered the way users interacted with their phones. The device boasted a 3.5-inch display, which was considered generous for its era, and introduced a multi-touch interface that allowed users to navigate through applications with intuitive gestures.
